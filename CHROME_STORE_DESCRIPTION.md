# Chrome Description for LODVault

## Short description
Save, study, and revisit Luxembourgish words from lod.lu with notes, flashcards, auto-save history, and local-only storage.

## Medium description
LODVault adds a personal vocabulary vault to lod.lu so you can save words, organize them into Favorites and Study, add notes, review them with flashcards, and export your collection — all stored locally in your browser.

## Full description
LODVault is a browser extension built for learners using the official Luxembourgish dictionary at lod.lu.

It adds a personal save layer directly on top of dictionary articles so you can build your own vocabulary list while you browse.

### Features
- Save words to **Favorites** or **Study**
- Turn on **Auto mode** to record visited words into Study and History
- Add your own **notes** for each saved word
- Search your saved vocabulary from the popup
- Review words with a built-in **flashcards** view
- Open a live **preview** page of your saved words
- Export your saved list as **HTML** or **JSON**
- Import a previous JSON backup
- Keep everything **local-first** with storage in `chrome.storage.local`

### Privacy
LODVault does not collect analytics, send your saved words to external servers, or require an account. Your data stays in your browser.
